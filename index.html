ABOUT ME

I’m Chandini Mohammad, a Graphic Designer from Hyderabad with a strong focus on web design, branding, and digital illustrations. I’ve worked across e-commerce, education, tech, and travel industries, Creating visuals that are both engaging and effective.

Skilled in tools like Figma, Illustrator, and Photoshop, I turn ideas into clean, user-focused designs. I enjoy collaborating with teams and delivering high-quality work that meets both creative and business goals.
